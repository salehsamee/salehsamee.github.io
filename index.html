Saleh Ahmed Samee

Saleh Ahmed Samee, professionally known as Saleh Samee, is a Bangladeshi guitarist, multi-instrumentalist, and composer. Based in Dhaka, he has carved a distinct niche in the South Asian music industry by seamlessly blending contemporary pop, rock, and electronic textures with the rich heritage of ethnic string instruments.

Widely recognized for his iconic Pink Panther Custom Stratocaster, Samee is a master of the Afghan Rabab, Mandolin, Dotara, Saz, and Oud. His extensive work as a session player and producer has made him a pivotal collaborator for Bangladesh’s mainstream icons and international music circuits.

🤝 Notable Collaborations

Saleh Samee has worked closely with the industry's most influential voices:

  • Habib Wahid: Long-standing lead guitarist for international tours and major national concerts.
  • Minar Rahman / Dilshad Nahar Kona: Collaborative session work and live performances.
  • Pintu Ghosh: Featured instrumentalist for special television and studio productions.
  • Emon Chowdhury: Collaborative work on film scores and nationwide promotional tours.
  • Imran Hossain: Featured performer for Digital Bangladesh national showcases.
  • Joler Gaan & Kureghor: Key instrumental contributor and touring member.
