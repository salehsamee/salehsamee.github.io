Saleh Ahmed Samee

Saleh Ahmed Samee, professionally known as Saleh Samee, is a Bangladeshi guitarist, multi-instrumentalist, composer, and music producer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. He is known for his work in ethnic fusion music, integrating traditional string instruments such as mandolin, dotara, and Afghan rubab into contemporary pop, rock, and electronic music. He has collaborated with leading Bangladeshi artists and has performed in international tours, national broadcasts, and major music productions.

Widely recognized for his iconic Pink Panther Custom Stratocaster, Samee is a master of the Afghan Rabab, Mandolin, Dotara, Saz, and Oud. His extensive work as a session player and producer has made him a pivotal collaborator for Bangladesh's mainstream icons and international music circuits.
