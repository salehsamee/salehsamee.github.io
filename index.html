Saleh Ahmed Samee

Bangladeshi Musician • Producer • Multi-Instrumentalist

About

Saleh Ahmed Samee, professionally known as Saleh Samee, is a Bangladeshi guitarist, multi-instrumentalist, and composer celebrated for blending contemporary sounds with ethnic string instruments.

Featured Work

  • Coke Studio Bangla (S3): Featured Rubab Artist.
  • Habib Wahid Band: Lead Guitarist for USA & Australia tours.
  • Collaborations: Kureghor, Tasrif Khan.
