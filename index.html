About Saleh Ahmed Samee

Bridging traditional Bangladeshi music with contemporary sounds

The Artist

Saleh Ahmed Samee, professionally known as Saleh Samee, is a visionary Bangladeshi guitarist, multi-instrumentalist, composer, and music producer based in Dhaka, Bangladesh. He is renowned for his innovative work in ethnic fusion music, seamlessly integrating traditional string instruments such as mandolin, dotara, and Afghan rubab into contemporary pop, rock, and electronic music.

His unique approach has earned him collaborations with leading Bangladeshi artists and performances in international tours, national broadcasts, and major music productions across the globe.

"Music is the universal language that connects cultures and generations. My mission is to preserve Bangladesh's rich musical heritage while pushing the boundaries of modern soundscapes."
— Saleh Ahmed Samee

Signature Expertise

Widely recognized for his iconic Pink Panther Custom Stratocaster, Samee is a master of traditional and modern instruments including the Afghan Rabab, Mandolin, Dotara, Saz, and Oud.
